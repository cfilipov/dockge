# OpenOLAT

- **Source**: https://github.com/OpenOLAT/OpenOLAT
- **Image**: frentix/openolat:19.1.6 (Docker Hub)
- **Description**: Open-source LMS from Switzerland (Online Learning And Training). Java-based with PostgreSQL. Popular in European universities.
- **Services**: openolat (Java/Tomcat app), postgres
- **Notes**: Official image by frentix (the company behind OpenOLAT). Supports SCORM, assessments, portfolios, course building.
