# Jitsi Video Bridge (Jitsi Meet)

- **Status**: created
- **Source**: https://github.com/jitsi/jitsi-meet
- **Image**: jitsi/web, jitsi/prosody, jitsi/jicofo, jitsi/jvb (Docker Hub, official)
- **Notes**: Simplified from the official docker-jitsi-meet repository. Includes the 4 core services: web frontend, Prosody XMPP server, Jicofo (focus component), and JVB (video bridge). Full deployment at https://github.com/jitsi/docker-jitsi-meet has additional optional services (Jibri, Jigasi, etc.).
