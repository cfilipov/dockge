# Yopass

- **Source**: https://github.com/jhaals/yopass
- **Image**: jhaals/yopass:latest
- **Description**: Share secrets securely via one-time URLs. End-to-end encrypted with OpenPGP, auto-destruct after read.
- **Services**: App + Memcached
- **Port**: 1337
- **Compose reference**: From upstream deploy/docker-compose/insecure/docker-compose.yml.
