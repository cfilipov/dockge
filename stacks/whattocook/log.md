# What To Cook?

- **Source**: https://github.com/kassner/whattocook
- **Description**: Recipe suggestion app that adapts to ingredients you have available at home
- **Architecture**: Java/Spring Boot backend with PostgreSQL
- **Images**: ghcr.io/kassner/whattocook, postgres:17
- **Compose reference**: Directly from upstream examples/docker-compose.yml
- **Notes**: Simple two-container setup. Add your available ingredients and get recipe suggestions. Manages recipe database with ingredient tracking.
