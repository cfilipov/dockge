# docassemble

- **Source**: https://github.com/jhpyle/docassemble
- **Category**: Polls and Events
- **Description**: Expert system for guided interviews and document assembly, used for legal forms and surveys.
- **Services**: docassemble (Python app), db (PostgreSQL), redis, rabbitmq
- **Notes**: Multi-component setup. The official image bundles many services internally but external DB/Redis/RabbitMQ is recommended for production.
