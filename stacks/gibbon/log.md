# Gibbon

- **Source**: https://github.com/GibbonEdu/core
- **Image**: crookiecookie/gibbonedu:latest (Docker Hub)
- **Description**: Flexible open-source school management platform. PHP/MySQL stack for managing students, teachers, timetables, attendance, etc.
- **Services**: gibbon (PHP app with Apache), mariadb
- **Notes**: Community Docker image. Gibbon is a school information system, not strictly an LMS.
