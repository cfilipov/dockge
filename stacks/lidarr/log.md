# Lidarr

- **Source**: https://github.com/Lidarr/Lidarr
- **Image**: `lscr.io/linuxserver/lidarr:latest` (LinuxServer)
- **Description**: Music collection manager for Usenet and BitTorrent users. Monitors RSS feeds for new tracks, grabs, sorts, and renames them. Auto-upgrades quality.
- **Ports**: 8686 -> 8686
- **Volumes**: config, music, downloads
- **Key env vars**: PUID, PGID, TZ
- **Category**: Media Management
