# Reaparr

- **Source**: https://github.com/Reaparr/Reaparr
- **Image**: reaparr/reaparr:latest
- **Port**: 7000
- **Description**: Cross-platform Plex media downloader that adds media from other Plex servers to your own. Multi-threaded download manager with intuitive UI.
- **Notes**: Formerly known as PlexRipper. Not affiliated with Plex Inc. Docker Hub image at reaparr/reaparr.
